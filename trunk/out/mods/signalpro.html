Signal Processing


Instructor(s)

Luc Deneire

Description

  • 2 ECTS

The main objective of the Signal Processing course is to introduce basic concepts signal processing (Fourier, z-transform) and their applications in networks. As such, the course also introduce the fundamentals of digital communications and give a quick overview of the main physical layers of wireless systems.

Matlab and/or Scilab will be used as a working tool to acquire the fundamental notions.

Assessment will be done partly by a final exam and partly by a project (homework) in matlab.

Prerequisites

Basic mathematics should suffice (including complex number theory, fundamentals of probability, …)
