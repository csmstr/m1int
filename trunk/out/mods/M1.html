Description:

This module has 3 main objectives:

  1. To give complements on how to write and speak French, which will be useful for your daily life.
  2. Provide the students with guidelines about how to give good talks from small talks to colleagues to a large audience of hundreds of attendees, and how to write technical and scientific documents from small white papers to international publications.
  3. Present the different stages of business creation, adopt appropriate managerial behavior and analyze various types of innovations.
