Program 2012-2013

(Slight changes will be done in 2013-2014. The new program will be soon available)

The Master 1 year consists of two semesters and allows you to earn 60 ECTS (European Credits Transfer System).

Each semester consists of core and optional modules. Each student must complete a Personal Project in one of the partner institutions (I3S or INRIA) for a total of 6 ECTS credits (equivalent to around 120 hrs fo work).

In addition, three courses Introduction to Research 1 & 2 and Scientific Writing aim at giving students a first experience in Research.

Semester 1, October-February (30 ECTS)

Core modules

24 ECTS

Concurrency and Parallelism 4 ECTS
Software Engineering 4 ECTS
Networking 2 ECTS
Foreign Language 2 ECTS
Computability and Logic 6 ECTS
Verification and Computer Proof 2 ECTS
Probability and Statistics 2 ECTS
Innovation & Entrepreneurship 2 ECTS

Optional modules

6 ECTS to be chosen among

Advanced Operating Systems 2 ECTS
C++ 2 ECTS
Introduction to Research 1 (winter school) 2 ECTS
Introduction to Research 2 (winter school) 2 ECTS


Semester 2, February-June (30 ECTS)

Core modules

22 ECTS

Temporal and Functional Analysis 3 ECTS
XML and Web 3 ECTS
Distributed Programming 4 ECTS
Complexity 2 ECTS
Personal Project 6 ECTS
Internet and Networks 2 ECTS
Image Synthesis 2 ECTS

Optional modules (only some modules will be opened)

8 ECTS to be chosen among

Scientific English Writing 2 ECTS
Cryptography and Security 2 ECTS

Geometric Algorithms 2 ECTS
