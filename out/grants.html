The Laboratory of Excellence LABEX UCN@Sophia provides scholarships to highly ranked students wishing to pursue studies at PhD level after the Ubinet master.

The conditions to get this grant are the following:

  • Excellent academic records
  • Have obtained a Bachelor diploma (or equivalent) from a foreign University
  • Follow the Master Ubinet program.
  • Do the internship in one of the LABEX’s research teams
  • Be eager to pursue PhD studies in one of the LABEX’s research domains.

Other grants program are available, depending upon your home country. Below we list some of these programs:

  • Eiffel applicable to all foreign students; before January 6, 2012
  • Averroes applicable to students from Maghreb
  • E. Galois applicable to students from Asia and Pacific; before march 1, 2012
  • Franco-Thai scholarship program applicable to Thai students; before february 19, 2012
  • Erasmus if you are from one of the partner universities
  • Emma for students from partner universities from Asia
