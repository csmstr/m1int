Protocol Engineering


Instructor(s)

Olivier Dalle

Description

  • 2 ECTS

This course gives an introduction to protocol engineering. The aim is to give an overview of the classical idioms and pitfall commonly found when entering the difficult task of building new protocols. This course is also an opportunity to introduce a few popular formalisms for studying protocols, such as Petri Nets and LOTOS. Some related tools, such as CPNTools, will be experimented on a few use cases during the hands-on lab sessions.

Programme

  1. Introduction to Protocols & Protocol Engineering
  2. Finite state machines
  3. Introduction to Petri Nets
  4. Acknowledgements
  5. LOTOS
  6. Reasoning on Petri Nets
