Program 2013-2014

(Slight changes will be done in 2013-2014. The new program will be soon available)

The Master 1 year consists of two semesters and allows you to earn 60 ECTS (European Credits Transfer System). Each semester provides 30 ECTS.

Each student must complete a Personal Project in one of the partner institutions (I3S or INRIA) for a total of 6 ECTS credits (equivalent to around 120 hrs of work).

Semester 1, October-February (30 ECTS)

Communication & Management 6 ECTS
Networking 6 ECTS
Calculability & Logic 6 ECTS
Software Engineering 6 ECTS
Winter School 6 ECTS

Semester 2, February-June (30 ECTS)

Parallelism 6 ECTS
Mobile Development & Embedded 6 ECTS
Resolution Methods 6 ECTS
Image 6 ECTS
Personal Project 6 ECTS
