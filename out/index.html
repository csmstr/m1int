Master 1 International in Computer Science

The international track of the Master 1 in Computer Science is designed for international students. Lectures are given in English. French speaking students are welcome, provided they have a sufficient level in English.

The Master 1 program corresponds to the first year of the Master degree studies. Upon successful completion of this program, the student will be able to pursue in one of the several Master 2 programs offered by the Master 2 IFI of the University Nice-Sophia Antipolis (UNS).

The Master 2 IFI offers several tracks in various fields of CS and IT (the details can be found here). Among them, two international tracks are entirely taught in English: Ubinet (Ubiquitous Networking and Computing) and CompBio (Computational Biology and Biomedicine).

Students of partner universities having an agreement with the University Nice-Sophia Antipolis are welcome and this track can also be validated in their country of origin.
